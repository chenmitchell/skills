# Signs of AI Writing

This reference lists common patterns and phrases often found in AI-generated text, based on Wikipedia's "Signs of AI writing" page.

## Common Phrases and Tells

- Hedging language: "It is worth noting", "At the end of the day", "It is important to remember", "Ultimately", "Generally speaking"
- Stock phrases: "In conclusion", "To be honest", "However, it's important to note"
- Rule-of-three lists: "First, second, and third", "X, Y, and Z"
- Transitional signposts: "Firstly", "Secondly", "Next", "Finally"
- Performed authenticity: "I hope this helps", "Feel free to reach out", "Let me know if you have questions"
