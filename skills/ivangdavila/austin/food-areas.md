# Best Dining Areas — Austin

## East Austin

### Why Go
- Highest concentration of excellent food
- Food trucks + restaurants
- Most creative and trendy scene
- Best for foodies

### Best Streets
- **E 6th Street** — Bar food, tacos, trendy spots
- **E Cesar Chavez** — Taquerias, food trucks
- **E 7th Street** — Newer developments
- **Holly/E Riverside** — Emerging

### Top Picks by Category
| Category | Best Options |
|----------|--------------|
| BBQ | Franklin, la Barbecue, Micklethwait |
| Tacos | Veracruz, Cuantos, Rosita's |
| Fine dining | Suerte, Dai Due, Kemuri Tatsu-ya |
| Cocktails | Whisler's, Small Victory |
| Coffee | Cenote, Fleet Coffee |
| Late night | Via 313, food trucks |

### Vibe
- Creative, hipster-ish
- Outdoor patios
- Casual dress
- Walk between spots

---

## South Congress (SoCo)

### Why Go
- Iconic Austin strip
- Great for people-watching
- Mix of casual and upscale
- Walking-friendly

### Best Section
- **Congress Ave (Academy to Oltorf)** — Main strip
- **Elizabeth Street** — Side street gems

### Top Picks by Category
| Category | Best Options |
|----------|--------------|
| Brunch | Café No Se, June's |
| Dinner | Perla's, Olamaie, Sway |
| Tacos | Guero's |
| Coffee | Jo's Coffee |
| Dessert | Amy's Ice Cream, Hey Cupcake |
| Drinks | Hotel San Jose patio |

### Vibe
- Tourist-friendly
- Instagram-ready
- Higher prices
- Outdoor seating premium

---

## South Lamar

### Why Go
- Restaurant row
- Entertainment district
- Mix of price points
- Less touristy than SoCo

### Hot Zone
- **Lamar between Barton Springs and Oltorf**

### Top Picks by Category
| Category | Best Options |
|----------|--------------|
| Sushi | Uchi (flagship) |
| Farm-to-table | Odd Duck |
| Tex-Mex | Matt's El Rancho |
| Asian | Sway, Ramen Tatsu-ya |
| Quick | Torchy's, P. Terry's |
| Drinks | Alamo Drafthouse, Saxon Pub |

### Vibe
- Local-focused
- Easier parking (relatively)
- Strip mall aesthetic in places
- Good happy hours

### Top Picks

## Downtown

### Why Go
- Convenience (staying downtown)
- Business meals
- Happy hour culture
- Late night options

### Best Areas
- **2nd Street District** — Upscale shopping + dining
- **Congress Ave** — Hotels, business spots
- **6th Street** — Late night, bars with food
- **West 6th** — More upscale bars

### Top Picks by Category
| Category | Best Options |
|----------|--------------|
| Sushi | Otoko, Uchi (newer location) |
| Upscale | Garrison, Péché |
| Asian | Wu Chow |
| Steakhouse | Perry's, III Forks |
| Happy hour | Many hotel bars |

### Vibe
- Business casual
- Hotel guests
- Convention crowd
- More formal
