## Austin City Limits Music Festival (ACL)

### Overview
- **What**: Premier outdoor music festival
- **When**: Two weekends in October
- **Where**: Zilker Park
- **Capacity**: ~75,000 per day
- **Acts**: 130+ across 8 stages

### Tickets
| Type | Price | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| 1-Day GA | $150-175 | Friday/Saturday/Sunday |
| 3-Day GA | $300-350 | One weekend |
| 3-Day GA+ | $600+ | Better amenities |
| VIP | $1,500+ | Premium access |
| Platinum | $5,000+ | Artist access |

### Selling Out
- GA sells out within days/weeks
- Resale market active (high prices)
- Sign up for presale notification

### What to Know
- **Heat**: October can still be hot (80F+)
- **Dust**: Zilker becomes dust bowl
- **Water**: Bring empty bottle, fill inside
- **Food**: Good food vendors, long lines
- **Lockers**: Rent one, saves walking
- **Entry**: Lines long at peak times

### Lineup Style
- Headliners: Major acts (Radiohead, Kendrick, etc.)
- Mix of genres
- Discovery stages for smaller acts
- Austin artists featured

### Local Impact
- Zilker area traffic nightmare
- Hotel prices spike
- Restaurants packed
- Great aftershows around town

## Other Music Festivals

### Levitation (Formerly Austin Psych Fest)
- **When**: Spring (April/May)
- **Genre**: Psychedelic rock, experimental
- **Vibe**: Dedicated fans, niche but excellent
- **Size**: Smaller, intimate

### Austin Reggae Festival
- **When**: April (Easter weekend)
- **Where**: Auditorium Shores
- **Vibe**: Laid-back, family-friendly
- **Cost**: Donation-based (suggests $10-20)

### Blues on the Green
- **When**: Summer evenings (June-August)
- **Where**: Zilker Park
- **Vibe**: Free concert series
- **Cost**: Free!
- **What to bring**: Blanket, cooler, friends

### Eeyore's Birthday Party
- **When**: April
- **Where**: Pease Park
- **Vibe**: Drum circles, costumes, hippie Austin
- **Cost**: Free (donations welcome)
- **What it is**: Weird Austin tradition since 1963

### Hot Luck
- **When**: May
- **What**: Food + music festival
- **Focus**: BBQ, chefs, live music
- **Founded by**: Aaron Franklin (Franklin BBQ)
- **Vibe**: Foodie heaven

### Old Settler's Music Festival
- **When**: April
- **Where**: Tilmon (30 min from Austin)
- **Genre**: Americana, folk, bluegrass
- **Camping**: Yes
- **Vibe**: Intimate, music-focused

## Non-Music Festivals & Events

- **When**: October
- **Where**: Circuit of the Americas (COTA)
- **What**: F1 racing + concerts
- **Cost**: $200-2,000+
- **Impact**: Biggest hotel price spike of year
- **Concerts**: Major acts (Taylor Swift, Ed Sheeran have played)

### Austin Film Festival
- **When**: October
- **Focus**: Screenwriting and film
- **Events**: Screenings, panels, parties
- **Industry**: Screenwriters gather here

### Fantastic Fest
- **When**: September
- **What**: Genre film festival
- **Focus**: Horror, sci-fi, action, weird
- **Where**: Alamo Drafthouse
- **Vibe**: Cult film heaven

### Fun Fun Fun Fest (RIP, but spirit lives)
- Was punk/indie alternative to ACL
- Ended 2015

- That energy still exists in Red River District

## Planning Around Festivals

### When Hotels Spike
| Event | Price Impact |
|-------|--------------|
| F1 (October) | 3-4x normal |
| SXSW (March) | 3-4x normal |
| ACL Weekends (October) | 2-3x normal |
| UT Football home games | 1.5-2x normal |

### When to Avoid
- **Mid-March**: SXSW chaos
- **ACL weekends**: Zilker area impossible
- **F1 weekend**: Southeast Austin packed
- **UT home games**: Saturday traffic

### When to Embrace
- **If attending**: Obviously go
- **If not**: Check for free spillover events
- **Locals**: Can enjoy peripheral benefits

## Festival Survival Kit

### Essential Gear
- **Sunscreen**: Texas sun is brutal
- **Hat**: Shade is valuable
- **Comfortable shoes**: You'll walk miles
- **Portable charger**: Phone will die
- **Earplugs**: Protect your hearing
- **Cash**: Some vendors cash-only
- **Empty water bottle**: Fill inside gates

### Festival Etiquette
- Don't push to front during set (arrive early instead)
- Watch where you step (people sitting)
- Be friendly to neighbors
- Clean up your trash
- Respect people's space and boundaries
