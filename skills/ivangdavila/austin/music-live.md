# Live Music Scene — Austin

## "Live Music Capital of the World"

Austin claims this title seriously. More live music venues per capita than any US city. Music is identity here.

### The Reality
- **250+ live music venues**
- **Free music most nights** — Many bars, no cover
- **Genre diversity** — Country, rock, blues, electronic, everything
- **Accessible** — You can see great music for cheap/free
- **Integration** — Music is everywhere, not just music venues

---

## How Live Music Works in Austin

### Free vs Paid Shows
| Type | Where | Cost |
|------|-------|------|
| Bar bands | 6th Street, East Side | Free (drink minimum) |
| Restaurant music | Guero's, many patios | Free |
| Smaller venues | Continental Club, C-Boy's | $0-15 |
| Mid-size venues | Stubbs, Mohawk | $20-50 |
| Large venues | ACL Live, Germania | $50-150+ |
| Arenas | Moody Center | $50-300+ |

### Finding Shows
- **Do512.com** — Best event calendar
- **Austin Chronicle** — Weekly picks
- **Showlist Austin** — Comprehensive listings
- **Instagram** — Follow venues
- **Walking around** — Especially 6th Street

## The Districts

### Red River Cultural District
- **What**: Block of historic music venues
- **Venues**: Mohawk, Stubb's, Cheer Up Charlie's, Elysium
- **Vibe**: Rock, punk, alternative, LGBTQ+ friendly
- **Best nights**: Thursday-Saturday
- **Threat**: Development pressure, venues fighting to stay

### 6th Street (Dirty 6th)
- **What**: Party strip, bars with bands
- **Vibe**: Young, loud, college-ish
- **Best for**: Walk around, pop into whatever sounds good
- **Cover**: Usually free, drink minimum
- **Warning**: Can get rowdy late night

### East 6th Street
- **What**: Hipper alternative to Dirty 6th
- **Venues**: Hotel Vegas, Volstead, Whisler's
- **Vibe**: Less chaotic, better music
- **Best for**: Craft cocktails + music

### South Congress
- **What**: Restaurant/bar music
- **Venues**: Continental Club, C-Boy's
- **Vibe**: More relaxed, older crowd
- **Best for**: Quality without chaos

## Austin City Limits (TV Show)

### What It Is
- Longest-running music TV show in history
- Tapes at ACL Live at The Moody Theater
- Free tickets available (lottery)

### How to Attend
1. Check acl-live.com for tapings
2. Enter lottery for tickets
3. Usually 2-3 per season
4. Free but competitive

### What to Expect
- Professional TV taping
- Multiple takes possible
- Standing room (dress comfortably)
- 2-3 hour experience
- Incredible sound quality

## Tips for Live Music

### Etiquette
- **Listening rooms** (Saxon Pub, Cactus Cafe): No talking during songs
- **Bars**: Background music expected
- **Tip the band**: Tip jars common for free shows
- **Buy merch**: Bands make money on merch, not Spotify

### Practical
- **Cash**: Some venues/tips prefer cash
- **Earplugs**: Venues are LOUD. Bring them.
- **Arrive early**: Good spots fill up
- **Stay hydrated**: Dancing + drinks = dehydration

### Cost Saving
- **Free shows**: Most bars, many restaurants
- **Early shows**: Often free/cheap before 10pm
- **Monday-Wednesday**: Less crowded, sometimes cheaper
- **Happy hours**: Some venues have music + deals

## Supporting Austin Music

### The Challenge
- Live music venues under threat from development
- Musicians face high costs of living
- Streaming pays almost nothing

### How to Help
- Go to shows (obvious but crucial)
- Tip bands at free shows
- Buy merch
- Tell friends about local bands
- Support Save Our Stages/NIVA efforts
