# International Dining in Bucharest

## Fine Dining

### Tasting Menu Excellence

**The Artist** ★★★★★
- Romanian-French fusion
- 7-9 course tasting
- Beautiful presentations
- Wine pairings available
- €80-120/person
- Reservations essential

**Kaiamo** ★★★★★
- Modern European
- Intimate setting
- Exceptional quality
- Floreasca location
- €70-100/person
- Book ahead

**Noua** ★★★★★
- "New Romanian cuisine"
- Local ingredients, modern techniques
- Garden-to-table
- Herăstrău area
- €60-90/person

**Joseph by Joseph Hadad** ★★★★☆
- Israeli-Mediterranean
- Celebrity chef
- Upscale casual
- €50-80/person

### Michelin-Level (No Michelin Guide in Romania Yet)

These would be starred elsewhere:
- The Artist
- Kaiamo
- Noua
- L'Atelier
