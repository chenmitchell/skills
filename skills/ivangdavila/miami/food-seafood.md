## Stone Crab Season

### What to Know
- **Season:** Mid-October to mid-May
- **How it works:** Claws harvested, crab released to regenerate
- **Served:** Cold, pre-cracked, with mustard sauce
- **Sizes:** Medium, large, jumbo (price increases)
- **Price:** $40-80/pound (market dependent)

### Best Stone Crab Spots
| Restaurant | Area | Price | Notes |
|------------|------|-------|-------|
| **Joe's Stone Crab** | South Beach | $$$$ | The original, legendary waits |
| **Truluck's** | Multiple | $$$ | Reliable, less wait than Joe's |
| **Garcia's** | Miami River | $$ | Wholesale prices, less fancy |
| **Monty's** | Coconut Grove | $$ | Waterfront, casual |
| **George Stone Crab** | Delivery | Market | Direct to your door |

### Joe's Stone Crab Tips
- No reservations for dinner (2-3 hour wait)
- Lunch has shorter wait (still 1 hour+)
- Take-out is same crab, no wait
- Joe's Take Away next door — smart move
- Season ends in May — go early in season

## Fish Markets

### Buy Your Own
| Market | Area | Notes |
|--------|------|-------|
| **Garcia's** | Miami River | Restaurant attached, best prices |
| **La Camaronera** | Little Havana | Fish + prepared food, cash only |
| **Fresh Fish Market** | Miami Beach | Small but quality |
| **Whole Foods** (Brickell) | Brickell | Reliable but pricey |

### What to Look For
- Clear eyes (not cloudy)
- Firm flesh (springs back when pressed)
- Mild smell (not fishy)
- Bright red gills
- Ask when it came in (same day is best)

## Keys Seafood Day Trip

### Worth the Drive
| Spot | Location | Drive | Specialty |
|------|----------|-------|-----------|
| **Alabama Jack's** | Card Sound Road | 45 min | Conch fritters, dive bar vibes |
| **The Fish House** | Key Largo | 1.5h | Hogfish, local institution |
| **Lazy Days** | Islamorada | 2h | Waterfront, Florida Keys cooking |
| **Robbie's** | Islamorada | 2h | Feed tarpon, fish sandwiches |

### What to Order in Keys
- **Hogfish** — Keys specialty, sweet white fish
- **Conch fritters** — Fried, with key lime mustard
- **Fish dip** — Smoked fish spread, crackers
- **Key lime pie** — The real thing, not green
- **Whole fried snapper** — Classic preparation
