## By Budget

### Budget-Friendly ($1,400-2,200/month)
- **Little Havana** — Authentic, character, transit access
- **Kendall** — Suburban, chains, need car
- **Hialeah** — Cuban, working class, far from beach
- **Homestead** — Far south, very cheap, agricultural

### Mid-Range ($2,200-3,200/month)
- **Edgewater** — Bay views, growing scene
- **Midtown** — Walkable pocket, Target/shops
- **Coconut Grove** — Character, trees, community
- **Doral** — Suburban comfort, Latin culture

### Premium ($3,200+/month)
- **Brickell** — Finance hub, amenities
- **South Beach** — Beach access, nightlife
- **Design District** — Fashion, luxury
- **Key Biscayne** — Island exclusivity
