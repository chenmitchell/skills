# Miami for Students

## Universities

### Major Universities
| University | Location | Enrollment | Known For |
|------------|----------|------------|-----------|
| **University of Miami** | Coral Gables | 18,000 | Medical, business, marine science |
| **Florida International University** | Multiple | 58,000 | Largest in South FL, affordable |
| **Miami Dade College** | Multiple | 100,000+ | Community college, transfers |
| **Barry University** | Miami Shores | 8,000 | Private Catholic |
| **Florida Atlantic University** | Boca (nearby) | 30,000 | Public, growing |

### University of Miami (UM)
- **Tuition:** ~$55,000/year
- **Vibe:** Private, well-funded, Greek life
- **Strengths:** Medicine, marine biology, music, business
- **Campus:** Beautiful Coral Gables location
- **Sports:** Hurricanes football (big deal)

### Florida International University (FIU)
- **Tuition:** ~$6,500/year (in-state), ~$18,500 (out-of-state)
- **Vibe:** Commuter, diverse, affordable
- **Strengths:** Hospitality, engineering, international business
- **Campus:** Main campus in West Miami, Biscayne Bay campus
- **Growth:** Research university, improving reputation

## Getting Around as a Student

### Without a Car
- **UM:** Campus is walkable, Coral Gables has trolley
- **FIU:** Shuttles between campuses, but car really helps
- **Metrorail:** UM has a stop, useful for downtown
- **Uber/Lyft:** Students use heavily

### With a Car
- Parking permits required on campus
- UM parking is expensive ($500-800/year)
- FIU parking more affordable
- Street parking in Coral Gables has time limits

### Bikes/Scooters
- UM campus is bikeable
- Citi Bike available near campuses
- Electric scooters popular for short trips
