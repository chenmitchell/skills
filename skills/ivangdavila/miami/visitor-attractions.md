# Miami Visitor Attractions

## Must-See (Worth Your Time)

### Art & Culture
| Attraction | Why | Time Needed | Cost |
|------------|-----|-------------|------|
| **Pérez Art Museum (PAMM)** | World-class contemporary art, stunning bay views | 2-3h | $18 |
| **Wynwood Walls** | Open-air street art museum, Instagram gold | 1-2h | Free |
| **Vizcaya Museum** | Italian Renaissance villa + gardens, beautiful | 2-3h | $25 |
| **Frost Science Museum** | Interactive science, planetarium, aquarium | 3-4h | $30 |

### Beaches (Ranked)
| Beach | Vibe | Best For |
|-------|------|----------|
| **Key Biscayne** (Crandon Park) | Family-friendly, calm water, locals | Families, kayaking |
| **South Beach (South of 5th)** | Upscale, less crowded than main SoBe | Sunbathing, people watching |
| **Bill Baggs** (Key Biscayne) | Lighthouse, nature, quiet | Escape tourist crowds |
| **Haulover Beach** | Part nude, part regular, wide | No crowds, natural feel |
| **South Beach (Main)** | Touristy, party scene | First-timers, nightlife |

### Neighborhoods to Explore
| Area | What You'll Find | Time Needed |
|------|------------------|-------------|
| **Little Havana** | Cuban coffee, cigars, domino park, authentic culture | 2-3h |
| **Wynwood** | Galleries, breweries, street art, brunch spots | 3-4h |
| **Design District** | Luxury shopping, architecture, free art installations | 2-3h |
| **Coconut Grove** | Bohemian village, waterfront, relaxed dining | 2-3h |
| **Coral Gables** | Mediterranean architecture, Biltmore Hotel, upscale | 2-3h |

## Skip (Tourist Traps)

| Attraction | Why Skip | Do Instead |
|------------|----------|------------|
| **Ocean Drive (sitting/dining)** | Overpriced, aggressive touts | Walk through, don't eat there |
| **Bayside Marketplace** | Generic mall with tourist markup | Brickell City Centre (free) |
| **Jungle Island** | Expensive, underwhelming | Zoo Miami ($25) |
| **Calle Ocho touristy restaurants** | Tourist prices, mediocre food | Go 2 blocks off main strip |
| **South Beach during Spring Break** | Chaos, crowds, price gouging | Visit Nov-early Dec instead |

## Free Activities

| Activity | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Art Deco Walking Tour** | Self-guided, South Beach (free maps at Welcome Center) |
| **Wynwood street art** | Entire neighborhood is open-air gallery |
| **Design District installations** | Rotating public art, always free |
| **Lincoln Road** | Pedestrian mall, people watching, street performers |
| **Brickell Key** | Walk around island, skyline views |
| **South Pointe Park** | Watch cruise ships, sunset views |
| **Venetian Pool viewing** | Can see from outside (swimming costs $20) |

## Best Times to Visit

| Time | Crowd Level | Weather | Prices |
|------|-------------|---------|--------|
| **Nov-Dec** | Low-Medium | Perfect (70-80°F) | Moderate |
| **Jan-Mar** | HIGH (season) | Perfect | Peak |
| **Apr-May** | Medium | Warm, some rain | Good deals |
| **Jun-Sep** | Low | Hot, humid, storms | Cheapest |
| **Oct** | Low | End of hurricane season | Cheap |

**Pro Tip:** Avoid Art Basel week (early December) unless you're here for that — everything is 3x price.
