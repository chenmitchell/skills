## Specific Costs

**Groceries:**
- Milk (gallon): $5-6
- Eggs (dozen): $5-7
- Chicken breast (lb): $6-8
- Beer (6-pack): $12-16

**Dining:**
- Coffee: $4-6
- Quick lunch: $12-18
- Casual dinner: $25-40
- Nice restaurant: $60-100+/person

**Transit:**
- OMNY weekly cap: $35
- Old MetroCard monthly: $132

**Utilities (1BR):**
- Electricity: $80-150/mo
- Gas: $30-80/mo
- Internet: $50-90/mo
- Renters insurance: $15-30/mo

## Hidden Costs

- **Broker fee:** 12-15% annual rent (often negotiable)
- **Security deposit:** 1 month rent
- **Moving:** $400-2,000
- **Laundromat:** $5-7 per load
