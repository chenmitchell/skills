# Manhattan Neighborhoods

## Financial District (FiDi)
- **Vibe:** Corporate by day, quiet by night. Historic + glass towers.
- **Cost:** $$$$$ — 1BR $4,500+
- **Transit:** Excellent — multiple lines, PATH
- **Safety:** Very safe (9/10)
- **Good For:** Finance professionals, remote workers wanting quiet
- **Food:** Delmonico's, Stone Street bars, The Dead Rabbit

## Tribeca
- **Vibe:** Ultra-wealthy, cobblestones, celebrity sightings. Loft living.
- **Cost:** $$$$$ (highest in NYC) — 1BR $5,500+
- **Transit:** Good — 1/2/3, A/C/E
- **Safety:** Very safe (8.5/10)
- **Good For:** Wealthy families, high-net-worth professionals
- **Food:** Locanda Verde, The Odeon, Nobu

## SoHo
- **Vibe:** High fashion, cast-iron architecture, tourist-heavy, designer shopping.
- **Cost:** $$$$$ — 1BR $4,500+
- **Transit:** Good — N/R/W, B/D/F/M, 6
- **Safety:** Good (7.5/10) — pickpocketing in crowds
- **Good For:** Fashion lovers, architecture enthusiasts
- **Food:** Balthazar, The Dutch, Fanelli's

## East Village
- **Vibe:** Counterculture, punk roots, dive bars, late-night. Young, edgy.
- **Cost:** $$$ — 1BR $2,800-3,500
- **Transit:** Good — L, 6, N/R/W
- **Safety:** Medium (7/10) — late-night bar fights
- **Good For:** NYU students, nightlife lovers, young creatives
- **Food:** Veselka (24hr Ukrainian), Ippudo, Momofuku
- **Bars:** Death & Co, McSorley's (oldest, 1854)

## West Village
- **Vibe:** Romantic, tree-lined, brownstones, LGBTQ+ history. Carrie Bradshaw vibes.
- **Cost:** $$$$$ — 1BR $4,000+
- **Transit:** Good — 1/2/3, A/C/E, L
- **Safety:** Very safe (8/10)
- **Good For:** Couples, foodies, LGBTQ+ community
- **Food:** Via Carota, Corner Bistro, Magnolia Bakery
- **Notable:** Comedy Cellar, Washington Square Park

## Chelsea
- **Vibe:** Art galleries (200+), LGBTQ+ hub, High Line, trendy.
- **Cost:** $$$$ — 1BR $3,500-4,500
- **Transit:** Excellent — 1/2/3, A/C/E, L
- **Safety:** Good (7.5/10)
- **Good For:** Art lovers, LGBTQ+, central location seekers
- **Food:** Chelsea Market, Buddakan, Cookshop

## Flatiron
- **Vibe:** Tech/startup hub, chic restaurants, Madison Square Park.
- **Cost:** $$$$ — 1BR $3,800-4,500
- **Transit:** Excellent — N/R/W, F/M, 6
- **Safety:** Good (8/10)
- **Good For:** Tech workers, professionals, upscale dining
- **Food:** Eleven Madison Park, Eataly, Shake Shack (original)

## Midtown East
- **Vibe:** Grand Central, corporate, UN area. More residential toward Murray Hill.
- **Cost:** $$$ — 1BR $3,000-3,800
- **Transit:** Best in city — Grand Central hub
- **Safety:** Good (7.5/10)
- **Good For:** Commuters, corporate workers
- **Food:** Grand Central Oyster Bar, Sushi Yasuda, Koreatown (32nd)

## Midtown West / Hell's Kitchen
- **Vibe:** Theater district, Times Square nearby, diverse dining.
- **Cost:** $$$ — 1BR $2,800-3,500
- **Transit:** Excellent — multiple lines
- **Safety:** Medium (6.5/10) — Port Authority area sketchy
- **Good For:** Theater lovers, foodies
- **Food:** Mercado Little Spain, Los Tacos No. 1

## Upper East Side
- **Vibe:** Old money, Museum Mile, elegant, conservative.
- **Cost:** $$$$ — 1BR $3,200-4,000
- **Transit:** Good — 4/5/6, Q
- **Safety:** Very safe (9/10) — lowest violent crime
- **Good For:** Families, museum lovers, quiet elegance
- **Food:** The Mark, Café Boulud, JG Melon

## Upper West Side
- **Vibe:** Family-friendly, intellectual, Central Park access.
- **Cost:** $$$$ — 1BR $3,000-3,800
- **Transit:** Excellent — 1/2/3, B/C
- **Safety:** Very safe (8.5/10)
- **Good For:** Families, academics, culture lovers
- **Food:** Zabar's, Levain Bakery, Gray's Papaya

## Harlem
- **Vibe:** Historic Black culture, Renaissance architecture, rapidly gentrifying.
- **Cost:** $$ — Average ~$2,600
- **Transit:** Good — 2/3, A/B/C/D
- **Safety:** Medium (6/10) — varies by block
- **Good For:** Culture seekers, budget-conscious, more space
- **Food:** Red Rooster, Sylvia's, Amy Ruth's

## Washington Heights
- **Vibe:** Dominican culture, "In the Heights" neighborhood, strong community.
- **Cost:** $ — $2,000-2,300 (most affordable Manhattan)
- **Transit:** Good — 1, A
- **Safety:** Medium (6/10) — improving
- **Good For:** Budget-conscious, families, authentic neighborhood
- **Food:** Dominican everywhere, Malecon
- **Notable:** The Cloisters, Fort Tryon Park
