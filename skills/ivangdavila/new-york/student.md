## Money-Saving Tips

- **30-day MetroCard:** $127 (vs $3/ride)
- **Groceries:** Trader Joe's, Aldi
- **Free museums:** MoMA Fridays, Met pay-what-you-wish
- **Library card:** NYPL = free museum passes

## Part-Time Work

### On-Campus
- Research assistant: $15-25/hr
- Teaching assistant: $18-30/hr
- Library/admin: $15-20/hr

### Off-Campus Tech
- Bootcamp TA: $25-40/hr
- Startup intern: $20-35/hr
- Freelance dev: $30-75/hr

### Gig Economy
- Tutoring (Wyzant): $25-80/hr
- DoorDash: $15-25/hr
- TaskRabbit: $20-50/hr

## International Students

- F-1 visa: 20 hrs/week during school
- CPT for internships (requires authorization)
- OPT: 12 months post-graduation
- STEM OPT extension: +24 months
