# NYC Tech Industry

## Overview

NYC is #2 global tech ecosystem ($621B value, 150 unicorns).

- 203,819+ tech jobs (up 64% since 2014)
- 25,000+ AI job postings (#1 in US)
- AI accounts for 35% of VC raised
