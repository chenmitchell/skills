# NYC Transportation

## Subway Basics

**Fare (2025-2026):**
- Single ride: **$3.00**
- Express bus: **$7.25**
- Reduced fare (seniors/disabled): **$1.50**

### MetroCard vs OMNY

| Feature | MetroCard | OMNY |
|---------|-----------|------|
| Technology | Magnetic swipe | Contactless tap |
| Cost | $1 new card fee | Free with your device |
| Weekly unlimited | $35 upfront | Auto-caps after 12 rides ($35) |
| Status | Phasing out (ends 2026) | The future |

**OMNY Weekly Cap (best deal):**
- Pay per ride → after 12 rides in 7 days ($35), remaining FREE
- Works with: contactless cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay
- Use SAME card/device for all trips to accumulate

## Bus System

- **Fare:** $3.00 (same as subway)
- **Free transfers:** Between subway and bus (within 2 hours)
- **Coverage:** Extensive, especially areas without subway

## Taxis vs Uber/Lyft

### Yellow Taxis
- Initial: $3.00 + $3.50/mile + $0.70/min (traffic)
- **JFK flat rate:** $70 + tolls = ~$85-95 total
- Hail on street or use Curb app

### Uber/Lyft
- Typical Manhattan ride: $15-30
- JFK to Manhattan: $70-150+ (varies with surge)
- Surge pricing 2-3x during peak

**When to use what:**
- Short Manhattan: Yellow taxi (no surge)
- JFK: Yellow taxi flat rate usually wins
- Outer boroughs: Uber/Lyft easier to find

## Ferries

### NYC Ferry
- **Fare:** $4.50 per ride
- **Routes:** East River, Rockaway, Astoria
- **Best for:** Scenic commute, Governors Island

### Staten Island Ferry
- **Fare:** FREE
- **Route:** Whitehall ↔ Staten Island
- **Best for:** Free Statue of Liberty views
