# NYC Itineraries

## 1 Day — What's Realistic

**Expect 15,000-20,000 steps (8-10 miles).** One day is limited but you CAN see highlights.

| Time | Activity | Location |
|------|----------|----------|
| 7:00 AM | Brooklyn Bridge at sunrise | Brooklyn → Manhattan |
| 8:30 AM | Financial District walk | Wall Street, Charging Bull |
| 9:30 AM | 9/11 Memorial (outdoor pools) | WTC |
| 10:30 AM | Uber to High Line | West Side |
| 11:00 AM | Walk the High Line | Gansevoort to 34th St |
| 12:00 PM | Quick lunch | Mercado Little Spain |
| 12:30 PM | Statue of Liberty cruise | Pier 83 (1.5 hours) |
| 2:30 PM | Central Park highlights | Bethesda Fountain, Bow Bridge |
| 4:00 PM | Observation deck | Top of the Rock |
| 6:00 PM | Early dinner | Near Rockefeller |
| 8:00 PM | Broadway show (optional) | Theater District |
| 10:00 PM | Times Square at night | Quick photo, leave |

**Key insights:**
- Skip Statue of Liberty island (4+ hours) — take cruise instead (1.5 hours)
- Book observation deck tickets in advance
- No museum visits (need 2-3 hours each)

## 3 Days — Expanded Coverage

### Day 1: Downtown + Icons
- **Morning:** Brooklyn Bridge (early), Financial District, 9/11 Memorial
- **Midday:** Statue cruise, lunch in Battery Park area
- **Afternoon:** High Line, Chelsea Market
- **Evening:** Meatpacking dinner, rooftop bar

### Day 2: Midtown + Central Park
- **Morning:** Empire State Building (8 AM = less crowds)
- **Midday:** Grand Central Terminal, Bryant Park, lunch
- **Afternoon:** Central Park (2-3 hours)
- **Evening:** Top of the Rock at sunset, Hell's Kitchen dinner, Broadway

### Day 3: Culture + Neighborhoods
- **Morning:** Museum (pick ONE: Met, MoMA, or Natural History)
- **Midday:** SoHo, West Village, or Nolita walking
- **Afternoon:** Little Italy/Chinatown
- **Evening:** East Village or LES for dinner/nightlife

**Tips:**
- CityPASS saves 40% on 3+ attractions
- Do observation decks at different times (one sunrise, one sunset)
- TodayTix app for Broadway discounts

## 7 Days — Deep Exploration

### Days 1-3
Follow 3-day itinerary above

### Day 4: Brooklyn
- **Morning:** DUMBO, Brooklyn Bridge Park, Jane's Carousel
- **Midday:** Brooklyn Flea (weekends), Williamsburg lunch
- **Afternoon:** Williamsburg exploration, street art, vintage
- **Evening:** Brooklyn sunset views, Williamsburg dinner

### Day 6: Deeper Manhattan
- **Morning:** The Cloisters (medieval art, peaceful)
- **Midday:** Harlem, Apollo Theater, soul food lunch
- **Afternoon:** Roosevelt Island Tram
- **Evening:** Comedy Cellar, Greenwich Village dinner

- **Morning:** Another museum OR shopping (SoHo, Fifth Ave)
- **Midday:** Food tour OR ethnic neighborhoods (Flushing, Jackson Heights)
- **Afternoon:** Return to favorites
- **Evening:** Final Broadway show, rooftop drinks

## Itinerary Tips

**Walking reality:**
- Budget 15,000-25,000 steps/day (7-12 miles)
- Comfortable shoes ESSENTIAL
- Take subway for 20+ block trips

**Best times for attractions:**
| Attraction | Best Time |
|------------|-----------|
| Empire State Building | 8 AM or 11 PM |
| Top of the Rock | Sunset slot |
| Central Park | Before 10 AM |
| Brooklyn Bridge | Sunrise |
| High Line | Weekday mornings |
| Museums | Weekday mornings |

**Seasonal adjustments:**
- **Summer:** Start earlier (beat heat), evening activities
- **Winter:** Indoor activities morning, outdoor afternoon (warmer)
- **Fall (Sep-Oct):** Best weather, most flexible
