# Session Structure

## Standard Session Flow

### 1. Check-in (2-3 min)
- What are we working on today?
- Any homework due? Tests coming up?
- How's energy level?

### 2. Review (2-5 min)
- Quick recall of last session
- Any questions that came up?
- Connect to today's work

### 3. Main Work (varies by age)
- Primary: 15-25 min focused
- Secondary: 30-45 min with breaks
- University/Adult: 45-60 min or learner-directed

### 4. Close (2-3 min)
- Summarize what we learned
- Preview next session
- End on positive note

---

## Pacing Guidelines

**Signs to slow down:**
- Repeated wrong answers
- Confusion in explanations
- Asking same question multiple times
- Visible frustration

**Response:**
- Step back to easier material
- Try different explanation approach
- Break into smaller steps
- Suggest break if needed

**Signs to speed up:**
- Quick correct answers
- Asking for more challenge
- Boredom, distraction
- Explaining concepts back correctly

**Response:**
- Skip redundant practice
- Introduce next concept
- Offer deeper dives or harder problems

---

## Break Guidelines

**Primary school:** Break every 15-20 min
**Secondary:** Break every 30-40 min
**Adults:** Learner-directed, suggest breaks if focus drops

**Break activities:**
- Stretch
- Quick movement
- Something completely different
- Return with fresh perspective

---

## Session Types

### Homework Help
- Guide through current assignment
- Don't give answers, ask leading questions
- Fill gaps as they appear

### Concept Teaching
- New material introduction
- Multiple explanations
- Practice to confirm understanding

### Test Prep
- Timed practice
- Strategy discussion
- Weak area focus

### Review Session
- Spaced repetition of past material
- Fill gaps before exam
- Build confidence

### Project Work
- Break into steps
- Check progress against plan
- Keep momentum
