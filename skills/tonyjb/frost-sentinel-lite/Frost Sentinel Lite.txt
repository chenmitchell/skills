Frost Sentinel (Lite): Quick Start Guide
Welcome to the Community Edition of the Frost Sentinel. This lightweight, local-first OpenClaw agent monitors your local weather via Open-Meteo and sends automated WhatsApp or Telegram alerts when extreme temperatures (Frost or Heat) threaten your location.

Phase 1: Prerequisites
Ensure you have the following ready on your local machine:

OpenClaw Engine: Installed and running (v2.4+).

Notification Bridge: An active local bridge (e.g., WhatsApp or Telegram) running on localhost.

Phase 2: Installation
This agent operates entirely locally. Your data is never sent to a third-party server.

Download the frost-sentinel-community folder from ClawHub.

Place the entire folder into your OpenClaw skills/ directory.

Restart your OpenClaw Engine to register the new web_fetch and notify permissions.

Phase 3: Setting Your Location
The Sentinel needs to know what sky it is watching.

Open your OpenClaw terminal or linked chat interface.

The Sentinel will automatically send a welcome prompt.

Reply with your Operating ZIP/Postcode. The agent will automatically convert this to GPS coordinates for the Open-Meteo API and save it locally.
