Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

Overview

This Whoop integration is for personal use only by the account owner. This application connects to your Whoop account to fetch and analyze your health and fitness data.

Data Collection

This application accesses the following data from your Whoop account:

  • Recovery scores and metrics (HRV, resting heart rate, respiratory rate)
  • Sleep data (duration, quality, sleep stages)
  • Strain and activity data
  • Workout information
  • Basic profile information

Data Usage

Your Whoop data is used exclusively to:

  • Provide personalized health and fitness insights
  • Generate daily summaries and reports
  • Track trends and patterns in your recovery and performance
  • Send notifications and alerts based on your metrics

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your private server. No data is shared with third parties, sold, or used for marketing purposes.

Data Security

Your Whoop API credentials and data are stored securely on your private infrastructure with appropriate access controls.

Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as needed for providing the service. You can revoke access at any time through your Whoop account settings.

Third-Party Access

No third parties have access to your Whoop data. This is a private, personal integration.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke application access at any time
  • Request deletion of stored data
  • Access all data collected about you

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in data practices. The "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly.
